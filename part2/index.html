Bacci Ball

Bacci aka "Bacci Bear" or "Bitchy" is the cutest little muffin you will ever see. He's a maltese so he is super tiny. He can be pretty "bitchy" sometimes considering he is now an old man pushing the age of 18 years old.(human years) But he has recently warmed up to me a couple of years ago.

I call him my adopted child because he was not intially mine. When my mom got married her husband Russ, had Bacci. So technically I did not raise him and I got him through their marriage. But that does not mean I don't love him all the same. He is pretty stand-offish but when he warms up to you, he lets you pet him and pick him up. In the most recent years we have noticed him becoming a little more friendly after watching Oreo get so much love and attention. If he really likes you, he'll come over when you walk in and give u a single lick. I find it relevant to a little kid coming up to you, and feeling forced to give you a hug and then running away. Same thing.

Oreo Mortelliti

Oreo Mortelliti is a 15 year old Boston Terrier. He is my child and I love him so much. When I was younger, like before middle school, I would tell EVERYONE about my dog. At my old school Strafford friends everyone in the class including the teachers would have to draw a picture of someting of your choice. Of course I said dogs. I guess I talked about Oreo so much that almost everyone tried drawing a boston terrier.

Oreo is very affectionate and I blame this on me...and he gets sad when you leave him. I think thats the worst part of leaving the house is that he gives you puppy eyes when you close the door. I always want to stuff him in my bag and take him wherever I go. He is also very good with children. I am the oldest out of my cousins so they torture him when they come over but he takes it. He's my little baby and I am so happy he's mine!

Location

You can find Oreo on a regular basis laying in the sun on the floor in our kitchen, or sprawled out across his bed. he sleeps most of the day away and gives a good 15 min a day to running around. He's old now, so he gets tired more quickly.

You can sometimes find him in the living room, where he knows he's not suppose to be. But I secretly let him in there with me to snuggle. (don't tell my mom)

Friendships

Oreo had a couple friends. He had one in particular best friend and his name was Max. Max would sneak over when he ran away from his house and they would play in our back yard together. Max was a Jack Russell Terrier. They were cute little friends and when he went missing his owners would always know where to find him.

Like I said in Bacci's information, Oreo and Bacci have a love hate relationship. Oreo will put up with all Bacci's misfits for a certaint amount of time before coming annoyed.But oreo rarely gets mad but when he does, Bacci goes running into his safe haven, (his cage.) Which is strange because usually dogs don't like their cages but Bacci does.

Funny Fact / Story

This is more of an embarrassing story on my part BUT oreo was my ever first dog that was mine. My mom didn't want to get another dog, (we had this dog named nicky but he was evil and like bit everyone) but we had a pet store around the corner so me and my little friends would go and play with the dogs. So I found oreo and we played together and he just like snuffled in my ear and cuddled me and I was sold. For some reason I started peeing my bed every night and then finally my dad was like lets go get him. So me and my dad went and brought home a Oreo and then after that I stopped peeing the bed.

So strange but it is a true story. I am so happy we did go get Oreo because he is the best dog ever for me. I have kind of turned him into a sissy but whatever he doesn't need to be tough. All the time he just wants love and affection. In our old house when he was younger and could control his bladder, I used to have him sleep with me in my bed. I would tuck him in under the covers and he would pass out like a human--so stinkin cute!
